You used ChatGPT, Claude, or Cursor to build a working prototype. It's exciting—until you try to scale it. Suddenly you're facing API rate limits, database bottlenecks, security vulnerabilities, and technical debt that makes every change harder.

This is the catastrophic forgetting problem in Gen AI development: the tools that got you to v1 can't get you to production. That's where Gritsa comes in. We pair your Gen AI tools with elite engineers who understand exactly how to transform rapid prototypes into scalable, production-ready applications.

Two ways to work with us

Choose the model that fits your needs—end-to-end product development or team augmentation. Both approaches solve the vibe coding fatigue problem with elite engineers who understand production systems.

Development-as-a-Service (DaaS)

We build your product end-to-end. From your vibe-coded prototype (or just an idea), we deliver a production-ready application. Full development, architecture design, DevOps, and ongoing maintenance—perfect for founders who want to focus on their business.

Developer-as-a-Service (DevaaS)

Augment your existing team with elite engineers on-demand. Starting at $800/month for junior developers with vibe coding tools, scaling to $3,000-5,000/month for senior architects. Embed directly into your team with daily standups and full transparency.
