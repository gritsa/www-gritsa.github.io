What makes Jiva different

Most AI agent platforms are closed-source chatbot wrappers. Jiva is built for real business automation with autonomous decision-making, error recovery, and production-grade monitoring.

Autonomous Workflows: Agents execute multi-step processes without human intervention
Decision Boundaries: Define what agents can decide vs. what requires approval
Error Recovery: Automatic retries, fallback strategies, graceful degradation
Audit Trails: Complete logs of every decision and action taken
Integration APIs: Connect to any system - CRM, ERP, email, Slack, custom databases
